Kaffe Foreningen brings together three distinct coffee shops — Espressia Eksklusivo, AromaÆstetik, and BaristaMaestro. Each shop represents a unique coffee experience, curated by passionate individuals. Espressia Eksklusivo, led by the adventurous Arturo, is a haven for those exploring the unknown in the coffee world. AromaÆstetik, under designer Isabella, transforms coffee into visual masterpieces. Meanwhile, BaristaMaestro, guided by traditionalist Carlos, offers a classic and authentic coffee experience.
